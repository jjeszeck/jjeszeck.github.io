I’m Jane Jeszeck, and I operate Jigsaw, a design studio in Seattle focusing on book and publication design for clients near and far. Give me a creative concept and I’ll enhance it, and in its presentation make it shine. I am always ready to take words and images to new places.

Why Jigsaw? Because I believe that the world is a puzzle with an infinite number of pieces, all of which fit if you can figure out how. As I see it, my job is to help people present ideas so that their pieces fit with ease and elegance.
